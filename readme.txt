== Description ==

Exchange views and clicks with other wordpress sites for free.

Get more traffic to your website without spending anything.

**Important:** This plugin relies on a third-party service, provided by the plugin author, to manage ad exchanges between websites. 

The following data is collected and stored on our servers for the purpose of displaying ads on other websites:
- Banner title and descriptions
- Site title, domain(s), URL, and language
- IP address of the user who clicked on the banner

This data is used solely to facilitate the ad exchange and ensure proper tracking of views and clicks. The data is not shared with any additional third parties outside the ad exchange system.

- [Privacy Policy](https://www.alcmidia.com.br/ads-exchange/privacy-policy/)
- [Terms of Service](https://www.alcmidia.com.br/ads-exchange/terms-of-service/)

By using this plugin, you agree to the collection and storage of the data mentioned above for the purpose of running the ad exchange. Please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for more details.
